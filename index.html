Benefits Abound!

Who Benefits, and How?

It's a Win/Win all around for the CAM Firm, their Managers, Community Associations, Developers, Homeowners, Local Businesses, Realtors and Event Vendors...Everyone benefits from Sentry LIFESTYLE Nex-Gen 2.0! Read More about these benefits below:

01

Community Management Firm

Higher contract renewal ratios...

Sentry Management, as a CAM firm, benefits from new robust revenue streams, enjoying less disruption in contractual agreements with higher renewal ratios among current community associations. Also, Sentry enjoys a prestige, standing out from its competitors by offering standardized add-on LIFESTYLE services.

02

Community Associations

Higher perceive property values...

Community Associations benefit through an increased social network of regularly scheduled events and activities that are offset in costs via sponsors and advertisers, which in turn become an added benefit for developers and real estate agents in creating a greater perceived value, thus selling homes more quickly and at the upper end of their actual value.

03

Local & Regional Businesses

Creates heightened awareness of their offerings...

Local and Regional businesses who offer goods or provide services to homeowners ( i.e. - Realtors, insurance firms, pool maintenance, etc. ) can have a direct targeted-market into the community association via the multiple LIFESTYLE media outlets ( i.e. - LIFESTYLE magazine and eblast ads, brochure/business card lobby placement, etc. ). Thus, creating a consistent and heightened awareness of their offerings.

04

Event & Activity Vendors

Spend less money marketing for new clients...

Select LIFESTYLE events vendors ( i.e. - caterers, bounce house, entertainers, etc. ) will provide 10% of their service fees as vendor assignment fees to Sentry in exchange for becoming members of Sentry's Preferred LIFESTYLE Event Vendor Network. Obviously, consistent contracts with all Sentry-managed communities will increase in profitability, allowing Sentry to spend less money marketing for new clients and more on improved services.

05

Association CAM Managers

Ability to offer a valuable suite of additional services...

Sentry CAM Managers will directly benefit from being able to offer a consistent suite of monetarily self-sustaining LIFESTYLE event and activity services to their community association residents. This will lower managment dissatisfaction among homeowners of communities being provided these self-managed Sentry LIFESTYLE Services.

06

Homeowners & Residents

Imbued with a renewed sense of loyalty

Homeowners will develop an abiding sense of loyalty toward their community, along with a deep sense of satisfaction in their elevated LIFESTYLE and renewed confidence in their management team and the decision-making process of their board of directors..

LIFESTYLE Media
Outlet Channels

CO-BRANDED LIFESTYLE MAGAZINES

LIFESTYLE Magazines - Community/Sentry co-branded periodical (monthly/quarterly) with full, half and quarter page advertising opportunities for local, regional and nation businesses, plus Neighborhood Business Network page placement. Magazine will be available for free in digital format and residents may pay to have a physical coffee table version delivered to their door with profits rendered as Melrose accounts receivables.

LIFESTYLE WEEKLY/MONTHLY EMAILS

LIFESTYLE Community Emails - Community/Sentry co-branded informational eblasts with advertising opportunities for local/regional/national businesses.

LIFESTYLE WEBSITES

LIFESTYLE Websites - Community/Sentry co-branded websites or LIFESTYLE website extensions that have advertising opportunities via preferred local realtor or service vendor tabs. The entire community website hosting and maintenance cost is easily offset by local business sponsorship and advertising.

LIFESTYLE EVENTS & ACTIVITIES

LIFESTYLE Events & Activities - 12 standardized events ( 1 major per month ) that are prepackaged including local event vendors that pay 10% of service fee to be a part of the Sentry Lifestyle Events Network. Sentry Lifestyle Activities will have 15 prepackaged fitness and social activities for all age ranges including pre-approved instructors and preferred activity vendors that pay 10% to be a part of the Sentry LIFESTYLE Activities Network.

LOCAL BUSINESS SYMPOSIUM MEMBERSHIPS

Realtor & Insurance Firms Symposium Networks - Local realtors and homeowner insurance agencies will pay a monthly membership fee to attend quarterly wine-tasting functions to meet with homeowners, plus have preferred business card and brochure placement within community center lobbies.

LIFESTYLE EVENT VENDOR NETWORKS

Sentry Preferred Lifestyle Vendor Network - Approved event and activity vendors would monthly pay a 10% of gross service receipts as Sentry account receivables or a flat membership fee. Preferred Vendor business card & brochure lobby rack placement - Approved vendors simply pay a monthly fee to rent a Lucite business card and brochure holder on an elegant lobby rack within the clubhouse facilities.

LIFESTYLE Nex-Gen 2.0
THE PLAN

An overview road map to beta project success - Yes, I said road map....

Standardization of all Sentry LIFESTYLE Services

All media outlets (LIFESTYLE magazines, eblasts, etc.) will have a standardize template format that ensures the upscale consistency of the Sentry LIFESTYLE brand while still providing personalization to match the brand of the community they are delivered to. Read further below:

ASSESS, INVITE, PRESENT & IMPLEMENT:

Sentry LIFESTYLE Services Group would assess all Sentry Management communities to pinpoint 3 communities (large, medium and small) with varying needs with which to begin a beta-test roll-out that would lead to possible successful case studies to be used in future promotion. These communities would be contacted in a very professional manner requesting an opportunity to provide a comprehensive presentation to their board of directors that would lead to a 1-year beta-test contractual agreement for the introduction of Sentry LIFESTYLE Nex-Gen 2.0.


1.) Large community over 1000 homes.
2.) Medium community under 500 homes.
3.) Small community under 250 homes.




FIRST STEP:

Community Infrastructure planning (budgeting/man-hours/accounts receivable process) and standardization of co-branded media materials prior to beta-test implementation of Sentry LIFESTYLE Nex-Gen 2.0.


FINAL GOAL:

Program evaluation consistency and continual community success tracking reports that allow us a full company-wide roll-out for Sentry LIFESTYLE Nex-Gen 2.0, complete with marketing case studies. At the end of the day, the paramount goal is consistent increases in profitability for Sentry Management, Inc.
